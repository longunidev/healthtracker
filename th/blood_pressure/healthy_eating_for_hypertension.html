Foods to Avoid for Hypertension

  • High-Salt Foods

    Examples include salted meat, salted fish, pickled foods, etc.

  • High-Fat Foods

    Avoid fried foods, animal organs, fatty meat, etc.

  • High-Sugar Foods

    Limit consumption of candy, cake, dessert, etc.

  • High-Cholesterol Foods

    Avoid foods like egg yolk, animal liver, cream, etc.

  • Caffeine and Alcohol

    Reduce intake of coffee, tea, cola, beer, wine, etc.

  • Processed Foods

    Limit consumption of instant noodles, fast food, canned food, etc.

  • Additional Information

    Note that while the mentioned foods are not absolutely forbidden, individuals with hypertension should try to consume them in moderation or avoid them to better manage their condition.

    For a heart-healthy lifestyle, consider incorporating more fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins into your diet. Additionally, staying hydrated and engaging in regular physical activity can contribute to overall well-being.
