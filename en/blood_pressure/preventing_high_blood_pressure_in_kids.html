Supporting Healthy Habits for Kids to Prevent High Blood Pressure

  • Nutrient-Rich Diet

    Encourage a well-balanced diet by providing nutrient-dense, low-calorie foods like fruits and vegetables. Substitute high-added sugar and high-solid fat foods with healthier options. Offering a variety of fruits and vegetables during meals and as snacks can make nutritious eating enjoyable for kids.

  • Active Lifestyle

    Support your child in achieving the recommended daily amount of physical activity. Explore a diverse range of age-appropriate leisure pursuits that make exercise fun and engaging for kids.

  • Maintain a Healthy Weight

    Monitor your child's growth patterns and stay informed about how obesity is determined in children. Utilize resources like the CDC's Child and Teen BMI Calculator to assess potential weight concerns in your child.

  • Lead by Example

    Be a positive role model for your child by adopting healthy eating habits and obtaining the recommended daily dose of exercise. Demonstrate the importance of a healthy lifestyle through your own actions.

  • Additional Tips for Parents

    1. Involve kids in meal planning and preparation to foster a connection with nutritious foods.

    2. Organize family outdoor activities to make exercise a shared and enjoyable experience.

    3. Limit screen time and encourage more physical play for a healthier lifestyle.
